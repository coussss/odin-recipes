How to make Ham Noodles from Ponyo

Description

Fans of Ponyo will remember Ponyo’s obsession with food, especially her love for ham. The umami dish doesn’t have an official name, but it goes by a few names – such as ham noodles, Ponyo noodles, and Ponyo ramen. Here’s how you can level up your instant noodles with just 2 ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles
  • water
  • 1 slice cooked ham
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Spring onion (Scallion)
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps to cook:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Transfer the boiling water in to a saucepan. Add the egg and more boiling water until the egg is fully submerged.
  3. Cover the saucepan with a lid and wait around 8 minutes, or more depending on how cooked you want the egg.
  4. While the egg is cooking, slice the spring onion in to small pieces and set aside.
  5. Slice the cooked ham in to halves and set aside.
  6. Once the egg is fully cooked, retrieve from the saucepan, remove shell and cut in to halves and set aside.
  7. Add instant noodles to a bowl, pour boiling water over the noodles and cover with a lid. Set aside for 3-4 minutes.
  8. After the first minute, separate the noodles and add the ham slices, hard boiled egg and spring onion. Cover with a lid again.
  9. Wait the remaining 2-3 minutes, and the dish is ready to eat!
